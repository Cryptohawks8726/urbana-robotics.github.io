Our Robots

Julie

2022

Rookie All-Star
2022 Greater DC Event #2

The goal of the 2022 game Rapid React is to score cargo into an upper and lower goal in the center of the field, and to climb and traverse a set of bars at different heights in the end game for even more points. The robot design the team decided to go with was a high and low goal shooting robot, along with a middle climb. This is achieved using an over-the-bumper intake design, a sophisticated shooter system that utilizes a combination of a Limelight to calculate the distance away from the goal as well as an adjustable hood to set different launch angles to precisely shoot from anywhere on the field by adjusting both the angle of the shot and the RPM of the shooter motor. The climb is a passive climber that can reach the middle rung simply by driving into it.
