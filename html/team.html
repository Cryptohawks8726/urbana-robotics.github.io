Meet The Team

Mentors

The supervising advisors of team 8726, who help out with equipment or provide suggestions to benefit the team.

Control

Control team (or Programing) is the last subteam of our technical branch. Those in this team are responsible for programming the code that drives our robot, as well as anything else that the robot is designed to do.

Mechanical

Mechanical team is one of the subteams in our technical branch. These members are incharge of building the actual robot, and making sure all of it's parts are functioning.

Design

Design team is one of the subteams in our technical branch. Members use software like CAD to create 3D renderings of how the robot is supposed to work and function, ensuring a swift, efficient, and easy build process for the other technical subteams.

Business

Business Team is one of our subteams in the non-technical branch. Business members maintain team funds, work on gaining sponsorships, and handle team finances.

Marketing

Marketing Team is another one of our non-technical subteams. Marketing team members work on team visuals, team merchandise, and team branding.

Outreach

Outreach is our final subteam in the non-technical branch. Members in outreach work with members of the community to help promote STEM and the principles of FIRST Robotics to those interested. Outreach also works to provide assistance in the form of volunteering to small businesses, keeping the community surrounding Urbana High School, and those in it, prosperous.
