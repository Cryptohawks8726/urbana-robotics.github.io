MEET THE TEAM
RAPID REACT

Mentors

The supervising advisors of FRC 8726, who offer mentorship and provide aid that benefits the team

Mechanical

Members involved in building, wiring, and assembling the robot, as well as ensuring safety

Control

Involved members write the code that the robot uses to move, operate, function at all. Members also are involved with website management and other programing projects

Design

Members using software such as CAD and OnShape to create prototypes and digital renderings of the robot prior to assembly

Marketing

Members work on team visuals and merchandise, as well team branding and public relations. Members also communicate between other FIRST teams, as well as the team

Business

Members maintain team funds, work on gaining sponsorships, and handle team finances

Outreach

Members outreach with the community to promote STEM and FIRST Robotics to those interested. Members additionally contact local businesses to offer aid
