Mentors

Mr. Joshua Holmes

Lead Mentor

Mr. Holmes is a teacher at Urbana High School, teaching Physics at a variety of levels, with a passion in engineering and robotics. Having led FRC team 3793 (CyberTitans) at Tuscarora High School, Mr. Holmes loves to inspire and work with the students. We are honored and gracious to have him lead this effort at UHS.

Mr. Francis Gardner

Technical Mentor

"After graduation form Clarkson University, I started work at Bechtel Power Corporation where I worked for the next 32 years. My last position was Project Engineering Manager for a large power plant complex. Prior to that I was Chief Electrical Engineer for a number of years. My favorite part of robotics is the prototype period. I enjoy watching students turn their ideas into models and helping them with the build. I feel the robotics competition is a great way to introduce students to STEM topics and hopefully interest many of them in careers in STEM related fields."

-Mr. Gardner

Mr. David Lillibridge

Technical Mentor

Mr. David Lillibridge has been working with the Urbana team since our inaugural year, and alongside Mr. Holmes at Tuscarora High with the FRC team 3793 (CyberTitans). Having made a career in engineering, Mr. Lillibridge now helps students interested in the robotics industry, inspiring and helping the next generation of STEM leaders.
